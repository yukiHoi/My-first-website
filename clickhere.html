About Us

Second Chance Luxury is a service for
buying and selling authentic
pre-owned luxury items.

Luxury brands are not solely about looking luxurious or appearing rich, but more about their rich
history and cultural significance. Second hand luxury not only reduces coats, but also transforms pre-owned items
into cherished and unique pieces with a vinatge appeal. Ever wished there was a shopping destination for
pre-owned luxury goods that was as safe and satisfying as buying new?
The founders had this wish and that's where the idea for Second Chance Luxury came from.
The company was formed with the following objectives and guiding principles in mind:

  • Widen the customer base for exclusive luxury brands by providing a trusted source for gently used luxury goods.
  • Only offer items that were guaranteed to be authentic and could serve as an affordable alternative to replicas.
  • Offer a return policy that matched or exceeded that for new goods purchased from authorized retailers.
  • Provide a way for owners to sell or trade-in their luxury items to a trusted source.
